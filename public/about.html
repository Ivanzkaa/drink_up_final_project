What is Drink Up! ?

When you want to try out a new different drink, but you don’t know where to start. You go to a bar but you can’t get the bartender's attention, you're left there with the indecisiveness and nothing to say when they ask you what would you like to drink. Not only does Drink Up can help you in a bar situation, but at home where you are entertaining your guests. That's where Drink Up comes in handy and in just a few simple steps you can confidently choose what cocktail you want.
